ChatPaste Privacy Policy

ChatPaste is a macOS menu bar utility that converts copied Markdown into clipboard formats that paste more cleanly into Outlook.

Publisher: JMS Effective date: June 26, 2026
ChatPaste works locally on your Mac. Clipboard content is not sent to JMS, Apple, or any third-party server by ChatPaste.

Data Collection

ChatPaste does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share personal data. The app does not include analytics, advertising, telemetry, account systems, cloud sync, or tracking technologies.

Clipboard Content

ChatPaste reads clipboard text only when you ask it to convert clipboard content from the menu bar or the app's fixed keyboard shortcut. Conversion happens locally on your Mac. ChatPaste writes the converted plain text, HTML, and RTF representations back to the macOS clipboard when available.

ChatPaste does not automate Outlook, simulate paste, scrape browser content, or send keystrokes to other apps.

Local Settings

ChatPaste stores local preferences, such as formatting settings, using standard macOS app settings storage. These settings remain on your device unless you choose to remove the app or its local data.

Third-Party Services

ChatPaste does not use third-party services, external APIs, advertising networks, analytics providers, or remote logging services.

Children's Privacy

ChatPaste is a productivity utility and is not directed to children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

JMS may update this policy if ChatPaste's privacy practices change. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact JMS using the support contact listed on ChatPaste's App Store product page.
